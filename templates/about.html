About Liza’s Cake House

Liza's Cake House is a local cake shop located in Siniloan that specializes in creating beautiful and delicious cakes for different kinds of celebrations. The shop offers customized cakes, cupcakes, brownies, and other sweet treats that are perfect for birthdays, weddings, anniversaries, and other special occasions.

The goal of Liza's Cake House is to bring happiness to customers by making cakes that are not only tasty but also creatively designed. Each cake is carefully prepared with attention to detail so that every celebration becomes more special and memorable. Over the years, the shop has become well known in the community for its quality products, creative designs, and friendly service.

Our History

Liza's Cake House was established in 1994 in Siniloan. It started as a small home-based baking business created by a passionate baker who loved making cakes for family gatherings and local celebrations. Because many people liked the taste and design of the cakes, the small business slowly grew through word-of-mouth and recommendations from satisfied customers.

As the years passed, the shop expanded its menu and began accepting more customized cake orders for events such as weddings, debuts, and birthdays. With dedication, creativity, and consistent quality, Liza's Cake House built a strong reputation in the community.

Today, after many years since it started in 1994, the shop continues to serve customers with unique cake designs and delicious baked goods. It remains a trusted cake shop in Siniloan, bringing sweetness and joy to many celebrations.
